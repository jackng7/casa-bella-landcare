Meet Matteo

I'm Matteo Hilton. A varsity athlete at the University of Victoria, neuroscience student, and founder of Casa Bella Landcare. I have 5 years of experience working in the field. Prior to landcare, I have 6 years of experience working at Applied Bionomics in greenhouse environments, ensuring the good health and quality of plants and small organisms, as well as orchestrating a small research project.

Whether it is garden care or a full invasive removal and native reintroduction, I approach each job with the highest level of detail, quality and work ethic. I believe outdoor space should feel like the sanctuary of your home, and I put everything I have into making that your reality.

If you're in Greater Victoria and are looking for landcare from someone you can trust and build a rapport with, I'd love to hear from you.
